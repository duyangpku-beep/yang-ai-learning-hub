Yang's AI Learning Hub

以人文为根，以 AI 为翼

I came to this world through liberal arts and law — shaped by literature, trained in reason, and grounded in the slow, deliberate practice of traditional Chinese calligraphy. These are not detours from technology; they are the very foundation of how I think, create, and build. Everything here was made with love — and AI.

In this hub I share the AI learning materials I found genuinely useful, the prompts that changed how I work, and the things I've built. If any of it helps you on your own journey, that's everything I could hope for.
